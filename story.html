Our Origin

A Vision of Pure Intent

Founded on the windswept cliffs of the Coast, Aethereal was born from a singular desire: to create a space where the architecture does not compete with nature, but bows to it. Our story is one of reduction, of finding the profound in the simple.

Every stone used in our foundation was sourced from local quarries, and every angle of our structure was designed to frame the shifting light of the Pacific. We believe that by creating silence in our surroundings, we allow our guests to hear themselves again.
